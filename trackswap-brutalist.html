04 — Platform

Core Infrastructure

Feature 01 — Supply

Hidden Venues Unlocked

Airfields, industrial lots, private karting tracks — venues sitting at 60% idle capacity that no platform has ever listed. TrackSwap finds them, onboards them, and makes them bookable. This is our core USP.

Feature 02 — Insurance

Insurance at Checkout

Per-session on-track damage cover and third-party liability, purchased with one click at the point of booking. Partnered with specialist motorsport insurers. The single biggest friction point for venue owners — eliminated.

Feature 03 — Compliance

Digital Safety System

Mandatory video safety brief per venue, completed in-app before arrival. Digital waiver with e-signature. All data timestamped and stored. Venue liability reduced. No clipboard, no manual sign-in sheet.

Feature 04 — Monitoring

Real-Time Noise Data

IoT dB sensors at venue boundaries, feeding a live dashboard. Auto-alerts before noise limits are breached. Enables venues in residential areas to stay open longer and take more bookings without compliance risk.
