Platform Features 04 Core Features
Infrastructure

Built for the Serious Driver

Every feature exists to remove friction — for drivers who want to focus on the lap, and for venue owners who want to focus on the track. Not an event organiser. An infrastructure layer.

01

Hidden Supply Unlocked

Airfields, industrial lots, private karting tracks — venues at 60% idle capacity that no platform has made bookable. TrackSwap finds them, onboards them, and puts them on the map.

02

Insurance at Checkout

Per-session on-track damage and third-party liability cover, purchased at the point of booking with one click. Specialist motorsport insurer partners. No broker, no waiting.

03

Digital Safety Compliance

Mandatory video safety brief per venue, completed in-app before arrival. Digital waiver with e-signature. All data timestamped and stored. Venue liability reduced. No clipboard needed.

04

Real-Time Noise Monitoring

IoT dB sensors at venue boundaries feed a live dashboard. Auto-alerts before noise limits are breached. Enables more venues to stay open longer without compliance risk.
