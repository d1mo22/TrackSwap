Platform Features

Infrastructure
for the Serious Driver

Hidden Supply Unlocked

Airfields, industrial lots, private karting tracks — venues with 60% idle capacity that no platform has ever made bookable. We find them, onboard them, and make them yours.

Insurance at Checkout

Per-session on-track damage cover and third-party liability, purchased with a single click. Partnered with specialist motorsport insurers. No separate policy, no broker, no delay.

Digital Safety Compliance

Every venue has a mandatory video safety brief completed in-app before arrival. Digital waiver, venue-specific hazard notes, and e-signature — all documented and time-stamped.

Real-Time Noise Monitoring

IoT dB sensors at venue boundaries feed a live dashboard. Venue owners see decibel levels in real time. Automatic alerts before noise limits are breached. More venues stay open longer.
